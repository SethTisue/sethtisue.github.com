Chicago Now: Introduction

Chicago Now lists upcoming performances of free jazz and free improvisation, avant-classical, electronic and experimental music, plus any rock shows that seem to fit.

E-mail submissions of concert dates are welcome. Send them to seth@tisue.net.

Lineups, showtimes, and prices are always changing, so always call the venue to confirm.

Updates to this web page are distributed weekly via the Chicago Improvised Music mailing list. The list has a home page at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/chi-improv/.
