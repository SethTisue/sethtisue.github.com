A documentary about Ken called Musician was released in 2007. It was made by Daniel Kraus as part of his “Work Series”. Follow the link for screening dates. A four minute excerpt is on YouTube. The documentary will be released on DVD by Facets Video in 2008.

Notes

On all recordings, Vandermark plays tenor saxophone, clarinet, and/or bass clarinet. On some recent recordings (2002 or newer), he also plays baritone saxophone.

I am in the process of gradually adding recording dates and locations and track listings; at present most albums have this information, but not yet all.

This a discography, not a sessionography; I don’t plan on expanding it to include not-officially-available material such as radio broadcasts, audience recordings, and unreleased studio sessions. If you want to put something like that together, feel free.

Some out-of-date web browsers, such as Internet Explorer, will not display track listing information correctly (punctuation does not appear). I suggest switching to a modern browser such as Firefox (or Safari for Mac).
