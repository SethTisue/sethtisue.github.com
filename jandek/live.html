It’s true

The first ever live Jandek concert took place in Scotland in October 2004. It was unannounced.

There have been 56 concerts total so far: 1 in 2004, 10 in 2005, 9 in 2006, 10 in 2007, 10 in 2008, and 16 so far in 2009, including the first-ever Jandek tour, of Northern Ireland. 3 more shows have been announced.

So far twelve shows are available from Corwood on CD, nine of them on DVD as well, with more CD’s and DVD’s believed to be on the way.

To the best of my knowledge, authorized recordings of all the shows are in the Corwood vaults. Nearly all of the shows were also officially videotaped.

Booking info

Corwood handles their own booking. They have a post office box and are listed in the phone book.
