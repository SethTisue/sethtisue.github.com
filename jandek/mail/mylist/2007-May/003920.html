I chalk it up to changes in his timbre over the years but also in artistic affectation. Jandek's music, despite its improvisational nature, is very considered tonally, and if he tunes his guitar in such a crazy way, might he also adopt different voices for different moods? After listening to You Walk Alone and Blue Corpse yesterday, I've also rejected the "Eddie" theory I'd had for a very long time. I now think it was just Jandek doing a fake "blues" voice, and overdubbing himself. But of course impossible to ever know for sure, unless the J-man grants a third interview.
