I've really been enjoying Aaron Goldberg's writing on Jandek's albums:

http://www.furious.com/perfect/jandek/jandekreviews.html

One particular interesting point comes up in the discussion of the acapella
albums, where Goldberg writes:

But the most interesting thing to note is the fact that the voice has
changed. *Obviously there was a long break in recording *– maybe for the
whole '90's? – but here Jandek sounds like a guy who's at least in his late
'40's, compared to the 20-30 something that appeared on nearly all his
previous recordings. (my emphasis)

Does anyone have any views on this? Was there a long break in recording?
