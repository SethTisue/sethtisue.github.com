I did pretty much the same thing. I wanted to add when the Telephone Melts 
but thought it was too long for a mix CD. Upon the Grandeur gets a pass on 
that point for being pretty and I'll Sit Alone and Think a Lot about You 
for sounding to me to be the natural companion to I Passed by the Building. 
Also the selections are biased against the past 8 years. It's designed to 
start as gently as possible and then go to darker songs. Like a musical 
black hole slowly pulling unwitting listeners, who were really enjoying the 
ride at first, past the event horizon and down into inescapable 
fascination/mailing list subscription.
