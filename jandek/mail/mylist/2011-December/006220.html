By special arrangement with Corwood Industries, there will be a Jandek performance on Friday 15 June 2012 at the Salvage Vanguard Theater in Austin, Texas at 8:30 p.m.
The Representative from Corwood will be performing with a group that includes percussion, electric bass, xylophone, viola, and clarinet; the musicians will be announced soon.The concert is produced by The Church of the Friendly Ghost and ticket information will be forthcoming.
The last Jandek performance in Austin was in March 2008, so it will be OVER 4 YEARS with no Jandek performance. That's TOO LONG!
Thanks to the COTFG and to Corwood for working hard to make this happen!
I'll keep the list informed as more details are made public...hope to see you all there.
