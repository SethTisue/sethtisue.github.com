Due to some scheduling conflicts among former J-night regulars, and a 
resounding lack of interest from Pittsburgh-area musicians, I'm going 
to open this up to recordings from anyone, anywhere.  Sure, the 
concept is that it should be a live show, but if I can't get enough 
live people to fill out the hour, then...it can't be live.

To preserve something of that live spirit, though, I'd like the 
recordings to be live to two-track: no edits, no overdubs.  The date 
of the broadcast is March 9, so we have time for electronic 
submissions.  Here's what to do:

Send me an email off-list.  Don't attach an .mp3 to it or anything; 
this account doesn't have that much space. Just send me an email 
describing what you're doing, and then we'll work out the best way of 
getting it to me.  Ultimately, .mp3 and .ogg are fine.

Best,
