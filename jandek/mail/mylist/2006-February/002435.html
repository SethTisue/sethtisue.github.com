Don't have much time to write at this moment, but did want to get the info out there:

'Newcastle Sunday' arrived today - the first Corwood 2 disc set! The cover is of a VERY old castle (outside Newcastle? Should I assume?) with NOBODY in front. Like 'Glasgow,' we have another person-free cover, only this time we're in the old country.  The discs look the same except one says DISC ONE and the other DISC TWO just below the center hole. Tracklist as follows:

DISC ONE
1. Depression - 8:11
2. Other End of Town - 5:26
3. Every Morning - 6:59
4. All of a Sudden - 6:57
5. Locked Up - 4:33

DISC TWO
1. Some Other Name
2. Telephone Blues

5. Shadow of the Clouds

Thoughts later, but obviously we have some REALLY depressed themes early on getting more mystical on disc two. Love to hear from others.
