I haven't had a working scanner in about two years, but here's what I think
you're looking for:

1. Canticle of Castaway remains the most recent release. I'm chomping at the
bit for the next one, as I think it will be the Toronto show, which I've
been dieing to hear since I saw it almost four years ago!

2. Here's the six albums prior to Canticle:
Hasselt Saturday 0797 (underrated piano/vocal album)
Helsinki Sunday 0798 (Piano/Harp and Audience Noise (yes, it's a sort of
instrument here and works to spur on the players))
Portland Thursday 0798 (Double CD also on DVD  - great DVD btw)
What Was Out There Disappeared 0799
Camber Sands Sunday 0800 (Obviously, this will be the next DVD)
Bristol Wednesday 0801 (Speaker Melting Double Pack)
Hope that helps!
