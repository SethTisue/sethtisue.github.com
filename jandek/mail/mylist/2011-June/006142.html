Yes indeed. Andrew hit the nail on the head. Recluse is an emotive description. There's a difference between reclusive and someone valuing their privacy. Andrew put together the gig in Hobart and I was fortunate to get the opening slot. There is indeed a mystique about all things Jandek, for me the jury is still out on whether the Rep cultivates this himself or it's purely down the fan speculation, perhaps a bit of both. And indeed he does have quite a presence in person. When he asked me in his deep Texan drawl to help him lift his amp onto the stage, my fanboy-ness told myself, 'well I can die happy now'. However a little after soundcheck I noticed the Rep dipping his hat and saying a polite 'M'aam' to my wife sitting in one of theatre rows. And later when he shook my hand and said thanks and that my music was 'unusual' (fanboy schwing!) I realised he's just a regular, but private bloke doing really interesting stuff and in a prolific way.
