I would guess that Jandek fans would be pretty all-over-the-map  
personal-listening-wise.  There seems to be a lot of stuff going on  
in Jandek's music that seems to open itself to Music Geeks.  I mean,  
my personal tastes are wide-ranging.  I'm into pop, noise, country,  
avant kinda stuff, and most things in-between.  Lately, I've been  
really digging Jill Sobule lately, but I've been in a Daniel Johnston  
mood lately, too, and I love Sparks.  Actually, i've been doing an  
mp3 blog for about a month where I post a cut a day by whichever  
strikes my fancy -- it's at http://revme.vox.com if you care to flip  
through.  (There's amazon links, but they don't have any kickbacks.   
They're just there in case people want to buy the album and want to  
support the artist.  Which is a good thing.  I don't get a dime,  
since, well, what all have I done, huh?)
