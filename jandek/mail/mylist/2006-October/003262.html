Currently/recently - Abruptum, Ulver, Jazkamer, Corrupted, Halo,
Agoraphobic Nosebleed and Destroyer 666.  Something you might like to
czech out cos you listen to Jandek, and you maybe asked that question
hoping you'd get some similar sounding stuff - Dead Raven Choir.  I
recommend the album Death To Dead Wolves as a starting point .  Nothing
similar to it though - just a general vibe.  A guy reciting old poems
over some evil acoustic guitar/basses/cellos.  They also do some fairly
caustic black metal noise stuff.
