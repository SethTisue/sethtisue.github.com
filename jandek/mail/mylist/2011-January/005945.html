Just wanna say dive in! His catalogue is littered with gems so it is hard for me to recommend only one or two albums. Is certainly safe to go with what previous people have suggested. 
Of the measly 15 albums I have, my favourites are:
Ready For The House (the first one - perhaps start here)
Blue Corpse 
On The Way (I'm Ready is possibly my fav Jandek song)
Chair Beside A Window
Glasgow Sunday 

It is a very exciting time for you my friend! I love discovering a new artist and finding all this cool stuff you have not heard before. 
Have fun sir!
