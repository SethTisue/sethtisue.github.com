I've seen a few shows at the Scottish Rite: Tony
Conrad, Gerald Pape with the Arditti String Quartet
and a experimental film festival. It is a nice place
to see stuff. There is a balcony which may be open for
the show. Seeing Jandek is one thing, but seeing
Jandek from the balcony... It is a Freemason hangout.
You can pick up a Freemason brochure. There is a
golden two-headed eagle above the stage.
