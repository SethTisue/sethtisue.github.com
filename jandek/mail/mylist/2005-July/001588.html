Someone on another board recevied an email about the
forthcoming Instal 05 (where Jandek played his first
gig in 04) and it looks like he has been booked to
attend again - just passing on here in case anyone
needs to plan in advance a visit


Instal 
Arches, Glasgow 
14th-16th October 

Instal celebrates its fifth year with a line-up of
very special performances. Pauline Oliveros, possibly
the most important composer to emerge from America
since the 1950s, has chosen Instal to make her first
ever UK performance. Instal is also the only European
date that gabbling, revolutionary hysterics the Sun
City Girls are playing this year, while lunatic
Japanese rockers Hijokaidan play the first gig outside
of Japan in their 25-year history. Jandek, who played
his first ever live set at Instal 04 is returning for
another crack, and the line-up also features Henri
Chopin, Chie Mukai and Black Boned Angel.
