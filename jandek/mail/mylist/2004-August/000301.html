> As I was introduced to this all pretty late in the
> game, I'm curious to know how long you all have been
> Jandek fans?

Since 1994... when I picked up a copy of Graven Image because of the
photograph on the front of the CD.  Took me a while to grasp what I was
listening to.  Confounded me, bewildered me.  Scared me even, just a
little.

Took a while before I became rabid, maybe a couple few years.  

Then I bought White Box Requiem when I saw it in a SF record store, and
that's when I began scooping up bits and pieces when I had the cash and
watching closely for the new stuff.  Finished my collection only a couple
of years ago, but glad I did.

Never did get a chance to buy any LP's, but have recordings of about a
dozen of them.  The subtle changes make it worthwhile.

Good question.  Interested to hear more about others' first times.
