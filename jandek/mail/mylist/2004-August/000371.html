Jandek's latest (Corwood 0775) is entitled "The End Of It All," and is
comprised of four tracks:

1. One Of Those Moments (20:15)
2. I Had'nt [sic] Been There Before (5:49)
3. They Don't matter At All (5:36)
4. I Met You (8:12)

The cover photo is a close-up on a solemn teenaged figure (presumably
Jandek) dressed in a suit jacket and tie, looking morosely away from
the camera.  The photograph itself is reminiscent of a ceremony of
some type, perhaps even a wake.
