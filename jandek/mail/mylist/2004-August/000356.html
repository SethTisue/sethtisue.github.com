I would second that - esp. if you could arrange the walk in an older area
of town on an overcast day.  Late at night (1-4 am) is also very good.
Jandek's music has a 'haunted' quality to me that is best brought out in
environments like this.

Also, I don't know if you're familiar with the graphic novels of the artist
Seth, but they've a wistful melancholic quality (if that's not redundant?)
that also goes well w/ Jandek's acoustic stuff and are well worth checking
out in any event
