Going through the logs of my site sometimes reveals some interesting 
search terms people have used to get to my site.  This morning's 
batch includes a search for Jandek and iTunes--rather than just 
looking for a free mp3, someone is looking for legit mp3s.  So good 
on that person for trying.

I don't think Janky will be cutting a deal with Apple anytime soon, 
but the implications are quite interesting, if one lets oneself dream 
a bit.   All 39 albums would be instantly available.  There could be 
a Jandek-branded iPod, a coupon for a discount on the entire ouvre, 
as well as a minimal inscription on the back (maybe just the P.O. box 
address!).  Maybe make it an iPod Photo, containing images of all the 
covers.  Hey, if it worked for U2...
