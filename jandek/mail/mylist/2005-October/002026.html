Yeah I paid the money for that show, too. Also agree that it cost a surprising amount, though it was worth every penny. Still, I don't know that I'd say he "enjoys making money" off his music business. (maybe he wanted to justify a new guitar?  Who knows?) And part of my point of saying "at cost" was taking into mind that he sends an awful lot of music off free (his choice, admittedly) and obviously covers any (admittedly small) costs surrounding the recording of his studio sets.  The pro crew that taped the Austin show (and had everybody do that weird "keep your hands in the air" thing at the end) might be part of why the tickets cost so much but I don't know.   If he got a pay day off the live set it's one of the few musical ones he's received of any amount and by this point he's entitled to that.
