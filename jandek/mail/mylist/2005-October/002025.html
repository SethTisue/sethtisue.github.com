In a word, yes. And yeah, it's going to happen in this day and age and I know some people will download it BUT in the case of THIS artist I would HOPE that everybody would purchase it as well.  That's not a knock, in other words, against downloading it (that's to each individual's conscience and I'm hardly innocent) BUT to suggest downloading it INSTEAD of buying it for an artist who personally pays for EVERYTHING on these sets and has been NOTHING but gracious to his followers (after all, he basically sells his discs at cost and the shipping's free - plus you may get a cool note with it) - that I find hard to believe.

Yes, I will gladly buy every live set he ever releases, period, and I can't imagine it being suggested that somebody would be foolish for doing so.
