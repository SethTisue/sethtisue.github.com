Jandek will perform at the Abrons Arts Center on Saturday, April 14,
2007, at 8:00pm.

Tim Foljahn (bass) and Jim White (drums) will back the representative
from Corwood on guitar and vocals. Foljahn is best known for his work
with Two Dollar Guitar; White, for his membership in Dirty Three.

The show is being presented by Tonic. The venue is at 466 Grand St.
(at Pitt) on the Lower East Side; Tonic's site describes it as a
"wonderful and comfortable theater setting". Tickets are $20 (plus
service charge), on sale now from TicketWeb.
