There was a band in the mid-70s that, well, I'd sware, was Jandek.  
But they went under another name-- Punkinheads. If you listen to  
their 46-minute song called Roam, you'd swear you were hearing a very  
young Jandek sounding like Bob Dylan sounding like Fred McDowell  
playing Metal Machine Music. Does anyone have this album? I had it at  
the time, but it was destroyed during a great flood. I bought the  
album through a mail order post box in Texas, but unfortunately have  
no recollection of the exact address. It was very extreme for the  
day. Please, if anyone knows this record, let me know. It's been  
bothering me since I attended Jandek's Instal concert.
