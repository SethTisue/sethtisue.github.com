"...that's interesting news, but it's not true that this will be the first
non-corwood release of jandek material, as a jandek song was included on a
non-corwood underground cassette compilation at some point in the 80s. i see
the tape up for auction from time to time on eBay, so it definitely exists,
and jandek did reportedly give his permission for
its inclusion, according to reliable sources."


Beyond that, there's a Jandek cut on "Songs in the Key of Z," the remix /
remodel of "Lavender" as "Lavender Hip Mob" by Lee Rosevere on Comfort Stand
Records (you can download it-- it's down near the bottom of the Comfort Zone
catalog at http://www.comfortstand.com/catalog.html ) and the forthcoming
(?) remix of "Your Other Man" by Secret Frequency Crew (a scan of Corwood's
note granting permission is at
http://grimshaw.jeff.tripod.com/BlueCorpseNote.jpg) But the live cut from
Finland may well be the first Jandek track to appear somewhere else prior to
it's Corwood release-- assuming it does...
