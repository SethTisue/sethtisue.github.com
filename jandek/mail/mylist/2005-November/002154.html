I think the opening notes of "Naked in the Afternoon" reperesent my fave
Jandek moment.  This may not technically be the best/most interesting thing
he did, but when I listen to it, it's still evocative of the first time I
ever heard Jandek, late one night many moons ago, and being quickly
captivated by the bare, haunted sounds - and not being let down after years
of absorbing the hype from Forced Exposure and sporadic kudos/derision from
scattered other outlets...
