Here they are... I will be as bold as to say this is the best Jandek album EVER... very dark, very forlorn, but pretty, some of his best guitar playing too.  In the background when the music takes breaks you can hear something squeak like a bird or a chair... lots of background noise.
 
and long silences at the ends of tracks
 
here are the words... they are magnificent
