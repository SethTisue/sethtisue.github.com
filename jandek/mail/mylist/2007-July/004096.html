According to this blurb on the Centro-Matic page, the Ft. Worth
show will be filmed:

http://www.centro-matic.com/

"Additionally, Will will be drumming for legendary Texas recluse,
  Jandek, on Saturday July 21st at Fort Worth's Rose Marine
  Theater. The show is scheduled to start at 7:30, and the lineup
  includes Ralph White on fiddle and banjo, Susan Alcorn on
  pedal steel, and Baptist General, Ryan Williams on bass guitar.
  This performance will be filmed by Lee Daniel, who has worked
  on several Richard Linklater films, the Townes Van Zandt
  documentary "Be Here To Love Me", and the Roky Erickson
  documentary "You're Gonna Miss Me". Tickets are available at
  End Of An Ear in Austin, Good Records in Dallas, and
  www.ticketalternative.com."
