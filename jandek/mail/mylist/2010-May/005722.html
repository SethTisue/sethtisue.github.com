I think living in a moon so blue has better material. SATC is ok,
to me its like living in a moon so blue's other boring sibling. Its ok
not horrible, same tuning and overall guitar/vocal sound. But the story telling
and lyrics and images are better on living in a mooon so blue. I would not put
that album either one in his 'classic' albums though, although personally living in a moon so blue  i really do enjoy. Crime pays for some reason and all in an apple orchard. But all in all
I can listen to this album and not get bored. SATC doesn't keep my listeners ADD from kicking in but its not awful.
