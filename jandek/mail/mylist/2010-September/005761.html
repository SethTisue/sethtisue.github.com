Here is some more information about the Ottawa show on October 3rd. 

Jandek will be performing with Jesse Stewart and Nicole Marchesseau at
Carleton University in the Loeb Building on the 9th Floor in Studio
A900 on Sunday, October 3rd at 2:00. The instrumentation will feature
percussion and two pianos tuned a quarter-tone apart. Tickets will be
available for $20.00 (CAD) at the door. E-tickets will likely also be available
shortly. 

Check out ottawasunday.wordpress.com for up-to-date information about the show.
