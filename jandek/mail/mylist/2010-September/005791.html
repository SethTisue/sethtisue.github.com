official confirmation of that yet.  (Maybe the next person who orders

http://www.networksolutions.com/whois/index.jsp it brings up the usual
PO Box and phone number.

provide a contact email address of some sort.  Domain contact addresses
typically get a lot of spam, so it's common to use a new address
concocted for that purpose.  So maybe it's monitored by Corwood and
maybe it isn't.  Or if it is, maybe it won't be any more once the spam
starts rolling in.)
