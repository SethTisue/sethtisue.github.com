And but so my animation of Jandek's "I Went Outside"
aired on my weekly animated TV show "Scott Bateman
Presents Scott Bateman Presents" this past Monday on
PlumTV.

The Jandek animation's online here:

http://www.batemania.com/bateman365/day073.html

I only aired it after receiving permission from
Corwood--see the official (and kind of chatty!)
Cor-espondance here:

http://scottbateman.livejournal.com/719996.html

PlumTV is a brand new TV network here in the US that
currently only airs in about seven markets--places
like the Hamptons, Martha's Vineyard, Aspen, etc. No
word on how Hamptons residents like Diddy and Mariah
Carey reacted to the animation. But if the next Puffy
record samples "I Went Outside," we'll know at least
one person saw it.

The hope is to eventually get the show licensed to a
basic cable network so more people can see it. Plus,
we're working on international distribution, so stay
tuned.

So, yeah--Jandek's network TV debut. Fun! I've been a
Jandek freak for years, so this was a very special
project for me. Hope everyone else likes it, too.
