How are you going?:) Please allow me to extend a warmest welcome to another 
member of the Jandek tribe. Just wanted to remark on the bloody brain on the 
bedsheets shown in the Jandek documentary. I don't think that the filmmakers 
meant to imply that it is a brain of someone Jandek has killed or anything. 
The way I interpret it is that (at the time the documentary was released) we 
didn't even know Jandek actually existed, or that the man on the record 
covers was actually Jandek. The only thing that we had available to us were 
the songs...that is, the contents of his brain. Therefore, the brain is 
simply a representation of Jandek's presence!:) How does this explanation 
sit with people?
