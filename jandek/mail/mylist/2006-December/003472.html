Hey, all,

Jandek performs in Indianapolis tonight, Saturday, December 9, 2006, at the
Harrison Center for the Arts, 1505 N. Delaware Street, Indianapolis,
Indiana. Just wanted to alert the list to the most recent personnel
information which was just finalized.

The evening's performance will feature upright bass and drums alongside
flute and viola. Performers include Lester "Lammy" Johnson of the
Indianapolis cult soul/funk group Ebony Rhythm Band, local improviser George
"Sparky" Smith, Liz Janes (Asthmatic Kitty) and Nathan Vollmar (Vollmar,
Half-Handed Cloud).

Show time is 7 pm sharp. Tickets are $17 at the door. Hope to see some of
you there.
