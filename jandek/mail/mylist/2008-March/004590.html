Description
This FREE performance at Lydia Mendelssohn Theatre in Ann Arbor,  
Michigan is Jandek's first appearance in the area, and a rare  
opportunity to catch this enigmatic performer. The man from Corwood  
will be backed up by Christian Matjias on harpsichord and James  
Cornish on trumpet.

Doors will open at 7:30, the performance beginning promptly at 8:00pm.
Seating is limited to 650, and there will be NO advance reserved  
seating. The performance is presented FREE of charge by WCBN-FM and  
UMMA.
