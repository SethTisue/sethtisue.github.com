I listened to Glasgow Sunday and I think it's the best Jandek album since New Town. The thing sounds a bit awkward though; I think Jandek was often being unfair to the musicians by not giving them more time to adjust to his changes. Still, they pulled it off way better than I'd have expected, so kudos to them. Also, it's worth mentioning that never in the history of multi-instrumental Jandek has the music been tight.
