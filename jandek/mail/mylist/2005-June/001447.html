I agree, YWA is by far my favorite Jandek album. The other one that gets play is One Foot in the North, but only for "Upon the Grandeur". Both that song and YWA are great music for a certain, um, psychedelic state of mind in which I often find myself, being a college student and all. For more low-key moods, Blue Corpse is great. "Only Lover" is just plain gripping.
