I've always thought Jandek's music similar to Skip James. Mr. James' guitar  
style is unorthadox and his tunes aren't your standard blues, but his voice is 
 the clincher for me. An eerie, floating voice that haunts you far after 
you've  listened to it. He's my pick when it comes to the blues (though Son House 
is a  very close second).
