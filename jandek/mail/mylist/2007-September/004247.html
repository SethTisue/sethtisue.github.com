I go back and forth between the two extremes, myself. It's good to mix up whatever Jandek I'm listening to at the moment. A live album, then spoken word, then live again, then "Graven Image" or something else from that era.



 


One of the best aspects of being a Corwood fan is that listening is an adventure. It always feels like you're discovering something new, every time you listen. So many subtleties, so much raw emotion. You can't predict him, musically or lyrically. When Jandek takes you to the dark places, you're never sure where you're going to end up. Me, I'm just glad to be along on the ride.



 


Amy
