Hello all,

I'm writing to let you know that tickets to the Jandek concert on 
October 6th at Grinnell College will be available starting September 
25th.  Tickets are free, and you will be able to reserve one by 
telephone by calling the Grinnell College box office:  (641) 
269-4444.  The box office is open weekdays, noon-5pm.

The lineup, barring any last minute changes, is:

the Corwood representative - piano
Jennifer Wohlenhaus - oboe
Skye Carrasco - violin
Israel Neuman - double bass

The concert is in Sebring-Lewis Hall, in the Bucksbaum Center for the 
Arts.  Doors at 7, concert starts at 7:30.  Tickets may go quickly 
(but then again, who knows).

The hall is quite nice acoustically, and it looks to be a rather 
unique performance (even for Jandek).

Thanks and feel free to write if you've got questions,
Daniel
