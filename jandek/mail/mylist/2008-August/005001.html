Although they don't seem to have them at the moment, I'm sure that
Volcanic Tongue can get them for you easily enough, though you'll
obviously pay quite a premium compared to ordering directly and may
have to wait till they next did a bulk order.

For a relatively small amount like that I would just send actual US $
in cash to Corwood - the exchange rate on the high street is good and
sending just notes through the post, if it's only $30 or so, shouldn't
be too much of a worry. Money orders generate expensive bank fees and
probably wouldn't be worth it for such a relatively small amount.

I haven't checked, but there may also be ebay sellers with Jandek
stuff that would allow paypal etc...?

I hope that helps.
