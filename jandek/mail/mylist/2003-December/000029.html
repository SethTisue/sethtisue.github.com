i love 'blemish' by david sylvian. however, i don't really hear any jandek 
in it. sylvian is far too emotionally detached sounding to be compared to 
jandek and derek bailey is far too precise and technically proficient 
(though it may not sound that way.)

it is my opinion that nothing sounds anything like jandek besides direct 
immitations and those even fall far short. this is perhaps why i love Him so 
much.
