Every once in a while a recording artist comes along
who reminds us that above all, music is about
self-expression. Jandek is one of those artists, and
now I present to you another:

http://www.cdbaby.com/cd/thropida3

He's a middle aged guy who makes music with a guitar,
keyboard, and drum machine. He's hopelessly ameuteur,
and quite Jandekian in his lyric and apparoach, though
the songs are happier in tone. whereas jandek brings
out a mysterious tone, this guy is just funny. chusid
would have a field day.
