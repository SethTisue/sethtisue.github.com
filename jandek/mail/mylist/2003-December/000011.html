Glad the list is back.

This must be a good time to mention that the "Jandek on Corwood" film was
shown here in Chicago at the Intuit Art Gallery a few weeks ago.  Very
enjoyable.  It was well put together with some interesting imagery matched
up to the music.  Factually, the filmakers did a good job of telling the
"public" history of Jandek - covering most of the major theories/events,
etc.  I didn't learn any new "facts" about Jandek, but some of the
commentators made some interesting and thought-provoking points.

Interestingly enough, Mr. Seth Tissue did not appear to be at the screening
- perhaps he is working on cultivating his own bit of mystery surrounding
his true identity.
