This reminds me of the covers to "The Living End" and "Somebody in the Snow"
that I "inherited" from a long defunkt radio station where I dj'd at the
University of Arkansas in 1993 when it closed. The music director had drawn
two giant X's through the songs on the back (indicating that NOTHING was a
good idea - naturally, I made "OM" my theme song and played many others). On
the front were little squiggles of all sorts similar to the cover here. I've
heard of many more like these. I can't believe somebody thought this was
authentic - I don't even think it's trying to be. Our friend might (on a
rare occasion) sign "Corwood" or even "Sterling Smith" but never "Jandek" -
goes against his idea of the project, and this is not his sense of humor.
