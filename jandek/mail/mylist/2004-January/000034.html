Me: wow.
Me: but you have nevr heard jandek...
Me: one second!
***
Me: listen to this!!!

You Walk Alone: jandek's latest single
Her: BWHAHAHA, this is farking hilarious!
***

***
Her: haha, I don't like the music, but it's hilarious.
