I don't know if this has been mentioned before, but I thought it was worth a
giggle.  On the DVD "The Wicker Man" (a brilliant and wonderful movie, BTW)
there's an easter egg in the "extras" section.  Use the right arrow key on
your remote until a question mark highlights on the illustration of the
wicker man.  Hit enter and you'll see an interview with Christopher Lee and
the film's director, hosted by "the mid-south's number one film critic -
Sterling Smith."
