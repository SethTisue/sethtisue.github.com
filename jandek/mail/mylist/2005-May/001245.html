The cover photo of 'When I Took That Train' was taken in London. The  
building to the left is the Mansion House and the railings in front  
are an entrance to Bank tube station. The building behind and to the  
right does indeed feature another tube station entrance, but the  
building itself is no longer there. The Mappin and Webb building was  
a City landmark dating back to the 1870s but was demolished in 1994.  
So this picture was taken at least in the early 90s. There would  
appear to be Christmas trees on the Mappin and Webb building, so I'm  
guessing December time. I can't quite make out the registration on  
the car to the right but the first letter which denotes the year of  
registration looks like a C, which was after August 1985. The van in  
front is newer than that as that sort of model wasn't around until  
the late 80s/early 90s. There's not much else to go on although I  
think the grey building at the extreme right was new in the late 80s.
