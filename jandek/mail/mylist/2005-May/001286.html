Right on, Dave.  Jungle's comments actually make me want to start making my
own Jandek mixes....LOL.

but more seriously - I believe very strongly in an artists' right to control
how their music is presented to the public.  I believe just as strongly in
the consumers right to do what they want (short of violating copyrights)
with the product for their own personal use, once they have bought it.  I
have some of my favorite Jandek songs in my mp3 player, 'cause I take great
pleasure in hearing "nancy sings" and "message to the clerk" mixed in with
all the other stuff I listen to.  Just the other day I heard "governor
rhodes" in between "a message to you rudy" (the specials) and "fly me to the
moon" (frank sinatra)...and it made me very happy to hear it.

Allen
