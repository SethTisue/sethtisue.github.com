The "treating him with kid gloves"-accusers are just as tiring as his most
protective fans.

To me, referring to him as 'Jandek' as opposed to 'SS' is purely out of
respect and accuracy.  Nobody calls Carrot Top 'Scott Thompson' except to
berate him (not that they can't while employing the CT moniker, of course)
or as background information, because intolerable comedy isn't Scott
Thompson's shtick - Carrot Top is.  And Carrot Top's shtick is intolerable
comedy.

Similarly, music isn't SS's shtick - Jandek is.  And Jandek's shtick is
music.  If I want financial or gardening advice I'll go to SS, if I want
music I'll go to Jandek.  Hell, if I want to talk about Jandek I'll write to
Sterling Smith, but I think he's made it clear over the past 30 years how he
wants the musician presented.  That's not reading into, that's just paying
attention.

Calling him by his name seems to be a worn-out exercise of in-group
name-dropping.  If we know who he is and it supposedly doesn't matter, then
why do we need to refer to him by his name?

Lauren
