from the "news" section of The Wire

Jandek, outsider music's most notorious enigma, comes
out of the woodwork once again to play three dates in
the US in August and September. The dates, his first
in the US, have been organised by UK promoter Barry
Esson, who first persuaded the reclusive Texan to play
live at Glasgow's Instal festival in October 2004, his
first live appearance in a ‘career' spanning a quarter
of a century. All three dates will feature the Corwood
Industries representative backed by a pick up group
put together from local musicians. Austin Scottish
Rite Theatre 28 August www.jandekinaustin.com; New
Orleans Tulane University Dixon Hall (with MV + EE
Medicine Show) 2 September www.wtul.fm/jandek; New
York Anthology Film Archives (with MV + EE & The
Bummer Road, Loren Connors) 6 September www.anthologyfilmarchives.org
