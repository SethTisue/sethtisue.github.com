Just as a heads up on the Brooklyn tickets, I'd recommend that
anyone in the New York area try to get tickets in person. I took
the day off work when the Anthology tickets went on sale, showed up
at 12 when Other opened, heard tickets were going on sale at 2, came
back at 1:20, and was first in line. Altogether pretty painless and
more of a guarantee than hitting refresh on Ticketweb a billion
times.
