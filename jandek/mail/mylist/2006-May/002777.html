It's hard for me to listen to "No Break" (from Chair Beside a Window) 
without laughing. ("You're nuts! You're nuts!") And then there's always 
"Comedy" (from Living in a Moon So Blue), where our man deadpans the 
immortal opening "Here's looking at you kid / You got the German Measles."

I was also always highly amused by the fact that he cut "European Jewel" in 
the middle on the first album, and then picked it up -- in almost exactly 
the same spot where he left off -- <i>three albums later</i>. If that's not 
someone who knows how to have fun, I don't know what is.

~Sam
