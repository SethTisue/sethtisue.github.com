I don't have any of the LPs, but I think it would be wonderful if 
someone assembled an archive of the vinyl releases, digitally 
transferred and in FLAC format.

And yes, this definitely isn't a vinyl purism issue -- the difference 
in sound between LP and CD versions is HUGE at times.  I'd especially 
like to hear the original Ready for the House, which I've heard is 
radically different.

A fair number of the original LPs are floating around as MP3s, in 
varying quality;  I've never seen a transfer of the Ready For the 
House LP, though.
