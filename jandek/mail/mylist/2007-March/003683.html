Well it's not like it would be impossible to figure out a Jandek song.  
There are people that transcribe Steve Vai and Allan Holdsworth songs, and 
people that score out full Wagnerian operas.  The songs are definitely 
adventurous but I don't feel that they are out of the league of 
transcibeability.(HEHHHEHE nice word)

If you cover a Lynyrd Skynyrd tune and you do it slightly differenly then it 
is still a cover song.  Jandek is no exception to the rule.  I have seen 
people cover Acid Mothers Temple and Glenn Branca, and I have friends who 
have covered Jandek almost spot on but even if they did it a little 
differently I would still call it a cover, and by the way an EXACT cover is 
still and INTERPRETATION of the ORIGINAL by definition because it is not the 
original.
