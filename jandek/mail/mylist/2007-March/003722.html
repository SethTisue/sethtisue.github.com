Single admission tickets will almost certainly be available, as the  
capacity is around 4-500. The Central Presbyterian Church is in the  
heart of downtown but thankfully just far enough away from the  
drunken 6th St district, 2 blocks east of Congress on 8th Street.  
Take the 11th Street exit and I would suggest leaving town early, as  
traffic (north and southbound) on I-35 is very heavy this week.
