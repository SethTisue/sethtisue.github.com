Thanks to Chris, I investigated the lyrics at Seth's site and found that
they were still from the first set that I sent. Last December 22, I sent a
complete set of corrections, but Seth was obviously in a hot tub somewhere
ignoring the crucial needs of the Jandek site ;). At any rate, here's the
lyrics again, with apologies for the mistakes in the first batch, and thanks
again to Chris for pointing it out.

Oh, I also want to applaud the finding of the dolmen photo. Awesome! If
you're as lost as I am on what all a dolmen is, wikipedia has a good entry
on it. Just enter "dolmen" at the search.

Also, I've fallen WAY behind on commentary, but I did want to say that my
"Glasgow Monday" DVD does not seem to have any synchronization issues, and
that both it and the "Austin Sunday" DVD are well WELL worth the purchase.
It's odd with "Austin," as I was at the show. The stationary track
replicates pretty close to what it was like to sit in the audience. The
roaming camera does a good job at capturing the nuances in the players,
including some interesting expressions. If you haven't ordered yet, do.
