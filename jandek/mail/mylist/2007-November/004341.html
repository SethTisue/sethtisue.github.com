He only has nearly two days of albums - about 44 hours at this point, I
think.  Heaven knows if waify models can do it, so can a few Jandek fans.

When I listened to the whole catalog, it took me about a week.  I took
breaks for all necessary obligations, but I didn't listen to anything else,
and all of my free time was spent with the music on, reading the lyrics.
I'd love to be able to listen to all of them straight through, no beraks,
but I don't think I could stay awake that long.

The Electric End is actually one of the more energizing Jandek songs for me,
in terms of how I feel during/after.  To me, Lost Cause is a welcome break
after a few rougher albums - I don't care much for the 'lounge era' myself.
