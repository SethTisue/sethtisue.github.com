Haven't had much of a chance to comment on this, but if you feel a person in
your life is entirely too cheery, this might even them out a bit.

It's actually quite lovely at times, really solid guitar playing in a
similar style to the last studio album. For what it's worth, I have no idea
where he goes in the first song, either...I'll walk into that (hill
something? sounds like he says "heeeeaaaaaaaalllllwwwssss" or something).
Anybody else?

Outside of that, I gotta point out how surreal these lyrics are, even funny
at times. Gotta love that.
