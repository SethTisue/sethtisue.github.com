Let me know if you do put another one out, I'd love to hear it. 

I think that it's a real shame that Jandek hasn't had a bigger influence over music. So much of what is produced these days just sounds like absolute shit compared to what Jandek does. It amazes me that people can listen to the same uninteresting crap over and over again, (Coldplay comes to mind for some reason), when someone like Jandek who engages in such fascinating musical explorations is so ignored. 

If people like Jackson Pollack and William Burroughs can gain respect and acceptance in their respective fields I just can't understand why Jandek can't. The whole project, not just the music, but everything from the album covers to the post office box, is just so brilliant.
