Heyy:)
 
Welcome to the world of Jandek...it's always exciting to see new fans coming to appreciate the genius of the Rep. If you like "You Walk Alone", I would recommend The Living End and Somebody In The Snow...they are closer to that album than On the Way. Lost Cause is also a good one to check out, although I haven't had a good listen to that one yet.
