Jandek at the Paradiso! I don't think it's been mentioned yet, so I thought
I'd put this out there for those lucky enough to be near Amsterdam. Jandek
will be playing (I think it's at some festival or another - anybody know?)
at the legendary Paradiso in Amsterdam on November 10th. If I can figure out
what festival it is (Google isn't being much help), that should also provide
some ticket information. As I know more I'll be glad to post it. I suppose
I'm especially excited about this (even though I can't be there) as the
Paradiso is my all-time favorite venue for live show recordings like, ever.
What's often considered the best Joy Division bootleg (and I think I'd
agree) was recorded here, and now Jandek's coming. Any more info would be
appreciated, though again I'll have to marvel at it from afar.
