Tim Strange filled in the names of the April 2009 NYC show musicians for
me: in addition to Biba Bell (dance, vocals), they were J.C. Kuhl
(saxophone), Rob Clutton (bass guitar), and Eli Keszler (drums).

What's interesting about this lineup is that none of them besides Bell
are based in New York and all of them had played previous shows: Bell in
Ann Arbor, Kuhl in Richmond, Clutton in Toronto, and Keszler in Boston.
So, apparently Corwood assembled a kind of all-star lineup for this
show.
