Saturday November 6th the Super Secret Lodge Show will be playing nothing 
but Jandek releases... not only that.. but we'll be trying to fit as many of 
the 38 releases into 2 hours that we possibly can... this means using as 
many playback devices as we can possibly get our hands on... CD players, 
Turntables, Cassette Decks, MP3 players, Mini-disc consoles, Phone lines, 
etc....

the special is titled "A Swamp Full of Jandek" and will attempt to see what 
arises when Jandek reaches some sort of "critical mass"... a Jandek wall of 
noise where bits and pieces poke through every so often grabbing at your 
ears like monstrous hands.

The show is on from 8 pm to 10 pm (Pacific Time) and can be listened to via 
the web stream provided @ http://www.kaosradio.org (or on the radio at 89.3 
FM if you live in around the Thurston County region of Washington State). I 
will also be, as per usual, archiving this show in downloadable mp3 format 
on my website for a period of a week.

If anyone would like to see the poster I made for this radio event... Here's 
that URL:
http://www.duckhugger.com/art/poster/flyer-ssls-11-06-04-jandek.jpg

I hope that you all get a chance to tune in!!
