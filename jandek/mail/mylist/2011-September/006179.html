Indeed I did, albeit briefly. Being the fanboy I am I was honored enough
when Sterling asked me to help him lift his amp onto the stage, but later
on after the gig, shaking his hand and thanking him for a great gig, he
said my set was 'really different, unusual'. Well I thought that was great
coming from him! He was very nice.
Must say both David and Heather were
super nice and made for a great Jandek show and the fact that I loaned them
my cymbals for the gig means a lot to me...nerdy as that is. My friend
Andrew and his wife who both organised the show spent quite a bit of time
with them all and I understand Sterling, the man behind the legend, seems
like a lovely fellow. 

As for a favourite album, hard to say
really...'Ready For the House' is right up there still. I really like
'Chair By the Window' too.
Kinda digging on the spoken word at the
moment...but I've only got 'Worthless Recluse'. 

Cheers,
Matt
