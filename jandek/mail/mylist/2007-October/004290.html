Yep, if you're anywhere convenient to Pittsburgh Pennsylvania this 
November 5, it's time again to polish up a Jandek cover and head to this 
year's Open Mic Jandek Cover Night.

What we know: to do the fifth one, we're moving to Te Cafe in Squirrel 
Hill, at the intersection of Murray and Beacon.

It's still November 5, and it'll be 7 PM.

There's room for one, maybe two people to play at the same time.  Drum 
kits, unfortunately, are out.  There will be low-powered amplification, 
which is all we need.  It's a small room.

Anyone can come and play, but you have to cover a Jandek tune.  I'll 
have lyrics available, thanks to Seth's site.

More info soon.  If you're interested in popping by, please let me know.
