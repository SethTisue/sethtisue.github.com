Good point, but Jandek isn't Batman. He's not some media-saturated character that gets licensed out to whoever has a clever idea. He's the persona of a particular musician (and even that notion is debatable). To put it bluntly, he's not Danen's character, so what business does Danen have writing about him? And I don't care if "Corwood" sactioned the project- it's a matter of artistic integrity. Let Sterling tell Jandek's story! As a fellow auteur of fiction I don't want to take a shit on your creativity, Mr. Jobe, but don't you have anything else to write about?  
   
  And just for the sake of stirring the pot a bit, I don't think Jandek could play "Johnny B. Goode" to save his life.
