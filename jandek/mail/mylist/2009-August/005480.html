On Wed, Aug 12, 2009 at 3:40 PM, Mark Greenberg <mark at mayfairrecordings.com> wrote:



You CAN make album sides that long but you will have to pay for it with thinner grooves and an overall quieter record. It's the "Get Happy" rule.



>From the Chicago Mastering Service website (http://www.chicagomasteringservice.com/vinyl.html):

"As a side gets longer, grooves must become thinner (i.e. quieter) to fit on the side........ as you come down in level, you move toward the noise floor of the medium and the pops and clicks usually present in a manufactured record will be much louder relative to the music. "
