Well, while the Mandy Moore thing is indeed interesting, I don’t think it’s so out of the blue when you consider that she just got married to rocker Ryan Adams, the solo artist who used to be in Whiskeytown. He has some pretty decent taste, and he likes some far-out country and blues, so I have a feeling that the Jandek connection/influence came through Adams, rather than Jandek somehow popping up in Mandy Moore’s radar. It’s still cool, but I thought I’d point this out. 

And in terms of Moore covering a song, I’d love to see her and Adams do a version of “Painted Teeth.”
