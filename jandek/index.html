News

Jandek makes a home stand at the Menil Museum in Houston, Texas on December 17, 2011, presented by Nameless Sound. Admission is free and the start time is early. (info)

The Alberta shows in October were canceled. “It is with great sadness that Bug Incision and Weird Canada announce the cancellation of both Jandek concerts this weekend in Alberta, October 21 and 22. The representative from Corwood was denied entry into Canada for reasons that were unforeseeable and beyond the control of Corwood, Bug Incision, or Weird Canada. The concerts will be rescheduled, with the same lineups and venues, at a later date, to be determined. Corwood, Bug Incision, and Weird Canada offer our apologies. Ticketholders: please contact bugincision@hotmail.com for refunds/details/etc.”

Corwood Industries now has an official site at http://www.corwoodindustries.com/. (Unlike the one here, the catalog there is kept current.)

As you may have noticed this site is long out of date. For now, follow the mailing list for updates.

Everybody knows one thing about Jandek, that nobody knows anything about Jandek. There is nothing but the music. Some find it offputting or unlistenable at first, but to open ears it reveals great depth, breadth, intelligence, feeling, and rewards for the listener. To learn more, read the About Jandek section.

Please send corrections and additions for the site to me by email or to 8A Leonard Ave, Cambridge MA 02139 (note new address effective September 1, 2009).
